About This Journal

The Journal of Applied Sciences is a peer-reviewed, open access journal that publishes original research and review articles in all areas of applied sciences and engineering. Our mission is to advance scientific knowledge and promote innovation through the dissemination of high-quality research with practical applications.

The journal covers a broad spectrum of topics including but not limited to materials science, mechanical engineering, electrical engineering, chemical engineering, civil engineering, environmental science, and interdisciplinary studies that bridge traditional scientific boundaries.

Aims and Scope

  • Publish innovative research with practical applications in science and engineering
  • Promote interdisciplinary collaboration between different scientific fields
  • Disseminate knowledge that addresses real-world challenges and technological advancements
  • Provide a platform for researchers from developing countries to share their work
  • Maintain high standards of scientific rigor and ethical publishing practices
