Submission Guidelines

Before submitting, please ensure your manuscript complies with our submission requirements and formatting guidelines.

Manuscript Preparation

  • Manuscripts should be in English and prepared in Microsoft Word or LaTeX format
  • Include abstract (250 words maximum) and 4-6 keywords
  • Follow the specific formatting guidelines for your target journal
  • Ensure all references are properly cited and formatted

Ethical Requirements

  • Research must be original and not previously published
  • All authors must have contributed significantly to the research
  • Disclose any conflicts of interest
  • Include ethical approval statements where applicable

File Requirements

  • Main manuscript document (DOC/DOCX or PDF)
  • Separate title page with author information
  • High-resolution figures and tables (if applicable)
  • Supplementary materials (if any)
